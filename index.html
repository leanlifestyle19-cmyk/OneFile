// questions

Fair questions.

Who's behind this?

One maker who got tired of business software that needs a login and a monthly fee for what should be a simple tool. I'd rather the tools earn your trust than my name — so try every demo before you pay a cent.

Is this only for bakers?

No. Bakers happen to feel this pain sharply, but the tools cover quoting, getting paid, tracking orders, running your day and winning repeat customers — useful whether you tutor, cut hair, fix aircon, shoot weddings or run a stall.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. Everything you type stays in your own browser on your own device. There's no server collecting it, because there's no server at all. That's the whole point.

Do I need internet or an account?

No to both. Once the file is on your phone or laptop it opens offline, with no sign-up, no password, no email.

What exactly do I get when I buy?

The actual file — an .html document you save and keep. Add it to your home screen and it behaves like an app. No expiry, no "your trial has ended."

Can I get a refund?

Yes. Since you can try the full demo before buying, there shouldn't be surprises — but if a tool isn't what you expected, just ask within 14 days.

Can you customise one for my business?

Yes — that's the "done for you" option. Tell me your trade and I'll set a tool up with your name, rates and look, ready to use.
