// questions

Fair questions.

Who's behind this?

A solo maker who got tired of business software that needs a login and a monthly fee for what should be a simple tool. The tools speak for themselves — try every demo before you pay a cent.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. Everything you type stays in your own browser on your own device. There's no server collecting it, because there's no server at all. That's the whole point.

Do I need internet or an account?

No to both. Once the file is on your phone or laptop it opens offline, with no sign-up, no password, no email required.

What exactly do I get when I buy?

The actual file — an .html document you save and keep. Add it to your home screen and it behaves like an app. No expiry, no "your trial has ended".

Can I get a refund?

Yes. Since you can try the full demo before buying, there shouldn't be surprises — but if a tool isn't what you expected, just ask within 14 days.

Can you customise one for my business?

Yes — that's the "done for you" option. Tell me your trade and I'll set a tool up with your name, rates, and look, ready to use. Get in touch.
